5G Jamming Simulation (UE -> gNB band n77)

This standalone model simulates a jammer targeting the pre-configured band n77 spectrum at the base station. As jammer power rises, fewer legitimate packets pass and total network capacity drops.

Educational offline simulation only - no live network traffic is produced.
